Privacy Policy — Aurora Nap

Last updated: 1 July 2026
Français · English

Aurora Nap is a napping app. It has no servers, creates no account, and collects no personal data. Everything you set or save stays on your device.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you enable it, Aurora Nap records each completed nap in Apple Health (sleep analysis and mindful session). This data is written directly to Apple Health on your iPhone; it is never sent to us and never leaves your device. You can revoke access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

Music (Apple Music)

With your permission, the Premium option reads the list of your Apple Music playlists and plays them during your nap, via Apple's MusicKit framework. This access stays local to your device: we never receive or store any information about your library or listening.

Web radio & Spotify link

If you enter a web radio URL or a Spotify playlist link, these are stored only on your device and used to start playback. Opening a Spotify playlist redirects you to the Spotify app, which is governed by its own privacy policy.

Your backdrop photo

The photo you choose as a backdrop (Premium) is copied into the app's private storage on your device. It is never uploaded anywhere and is deleted with the app.

Purchases

The Premium purchase is handled by Apple through the App Store. We never see or keep your payment information.

What we do not do

No accounts, no advertising, no trackers, no analytics, no data resale. Aurora Nap contains no third-party tracking kit.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated; the date at the top of the page indicates the latest revision.
